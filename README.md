# Retail Store Product Sales Review Analysis

A comprehensive Excel-based analysis of retail product sales data to generate actionable insights for sales improvement and marketing strategy optimization.

## Project Overview

This project analyzes 100 transactions across four regions, examining product categories, sales representatives, and temporal patterns to identify revenue opportunities and operational efficiencies.

## Data Cleaning Process

### Issues Addressed

- Removed unnecessary punctuation (periods, commas)
- Corrected spacing using TRIM() and PROPER() functions
- Fixed spelling errors in regions, products, and dates
- Standardized month abbreviations (amr→mar, jna→jan, par→apr)
- Corrected regional names (Norht→North, Eats→East)
- Fixed product names (Speake→Speaker, Computres→Computers)

## Strategic Recommendations

1. **Replicate East Region Success:** Study and implement East's high-volume strategies in underperforming regions
2. **Leverage West Region Margins:** Apply premium positioning tactics to improve profit per unit across other regions
3. **Product Focus:** Concentrate marketing on proven winners (Tablet T8, TV 43Q, Laptop A15)
4. **Sales Training:** Implement cross-training based on Peter Obi's profit generation and Chinedu Nwosu's volume techniques
5. **Investigate February Performance:** Analyze and replicate factors driving February's exceptional revenue spike
6. **Category Development:** Evaluate viability of underperforming categories (Audio, Photography, Peripherals)

## Conclusion
This retail store demonstrates strong foundational performance with clear market leaders in both geography (East region), product categories (Mobiles), and individual products (Tablet T8). The business has capable sales representatives with varying strengths, and the total profit of ₦302,680,445 from 1,141 units indicates a healthy average transaction value. The primary opportunities lie in understanding and replicating success patterns (East's volume, West's margins, Peter's profit generation, February's revenue spike) while addressing underperformance in specific categories and regions. Strategic focus should balance between maximizing the existing strengths and systematically improving weak areas through targeted interventions.
